GI vs GL

Glycemic Index (GI) measures how fast a food raises blood sugar. Glycemic Load (GL) considers both speed AND quantity you eat - giving a complete picture.

favorite

Why Track GL?

Steady blood sugar means stable energy, better focus, and easier weight management. High GL meals cause energy crashes and cravings.
